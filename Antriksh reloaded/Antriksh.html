About Antriksh Club 🪐

The Antriksh Club at VIT Pune is dedicated to exploring the cosmos and understanding the wonders of our universe. Our club organizes star-gazing nights, space exploration workshops, and invites guest speakers from the field of astronomy.

Our mission is to bring together students with a shared passion for the stars and to deepen our understanding of the vast universe we live in.

Our Mission 🛰️

Our mission at the Antriksh Club is to inspire a passion for astronomy and space sciences among students. We strive to build a collaborative community where members can explore the wonders of the cosmos, delve into groundbreaking discoveries, and deepen our collective understanding of the universe. Through interactive activities, stargazing sessions, and thought-provoking discussions, we aim to make astronomy accessible, engaging, and meaningful for all. Our goal is to foster curiosity, broaden perspectives, and remind ourselves of the awe-inspiring scale of the cosmos and our unique place within it.
